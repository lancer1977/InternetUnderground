SEAQUEST

Every SeaQuest enthusiast should see this site and donate more info to make it grow. A good base is constructed: sound samples, nice water background, very brief episode synopses but no images. Half of the appeal of the show to begin with was that it looked so cool. Now that the show has been given the network ax, what better time to preserve its contents on the Web. Don't get us wrong. We are not encouraging graphic explosions; sometimes we just want more. It's the human condition. Maybe just one picture of the UEO cast -- please? More talking dolphin audio files couldn't hurt; after all, this is the U.S. and we did nominate Babe for picture of the year.
