THE TERMINATOR

From one of the many creators of the game "Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon" comes "The Terminator." Input an actor's name -- any actor's name -- and The Terminatory will search a database until it comes up with a string of people who appear in films together, all leading up, or linking to, the Terminator himself, Arnold Schwarzenegger. The program seems to consistently match your selection up within two to three degrees. This program just proves that through a powerful database, seemingly coincidental relationships can be found between almost anything.
