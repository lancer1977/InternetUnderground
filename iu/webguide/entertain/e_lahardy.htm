LAUREL AND HARDY

This is a content-rich Web site we really dug until we found ourselves mesmerized by the background of the pages -- they were all a lurid shade of green. We were haunted by it from the start. At first we thought it was a grassy sort of shade, kind of nice even. Then we began arguing over the green. Then it dawned on us; the green was controlling our lives and tearing us apart. We eventually learned to overcome our feelings for the green by reading through the text. The Laurel and Hardy film guide and image gallery are quite impressive. The listing of the L&H films is extensive and chronological. Perhaps the most useful element is the listing of show times for the various films. Back to that green...
