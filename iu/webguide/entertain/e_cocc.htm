CIRCLE OF CRITICS' CAFÉ

Going to see a movie is a big commitment these days at $7 a pop. If you are wary of risking it all one person's opinion, check out the Circle of Critics, four Seattle guys who offer their varying opinions on current releases. Each has distinct tastes, ranging from the ultra-violent to the urbane to the semi-slack, so be sure to get acquainted with their profiles and discover with whom you identify. They are actually smart and funny, and it's amusing to listen to the sound files of their post-film debates with each other. The prevailing view is that plot is more important than character, so be sure that that's your priority, too.
