HORROR WEB

Amazing mix of horror film resources. The horror timeline is scholarly in scope, but amusing in execution, ranging from a history of horror starting from 1235, a history of horror special effects and a history of censorship, all cross-referenced and annotated. There's also a library of sound clips and pictures from various horror movies such as Halloween and The Thing, which contain a short text description before downloading. You'll also find links to horror literature on the Web -- both professional and amateur. The creator of this site knows his subject matter well, and holds forth admirably.
