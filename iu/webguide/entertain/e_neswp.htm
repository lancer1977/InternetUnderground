THE NEVERENDING STORY WEB PAGE

The main character of this story, Bastian, spent his time searching for reality in a world that was very much a fantasy. He began to have trouble distinguishing between the two. This is the perfect Internet story. The Web is Fantasia and you have gotten lost in it. Subsequently, you must "find your true identity in the real world." This site explains The Neverending Story, as told in the original novel by Michael Ende, and links to information about the films, from the original German version, Die Unendliche Geschichte, to the American rendition created in 1990. Includes links to other similar sites and book/album cover images.
