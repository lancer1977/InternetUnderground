JIM CARREY, A TRIBUTE

Dedicated to the actor you either love or hate, this site is filled with the usual content: pictures and sounds. If you want to look at Jim Carrey images or hear his slapstick one-liners such as, "Allll righty then" and "Smokin'!" until you can't stand any more, this is the site for you. The Carrey FAQ will answer some questions about the actor best known for his crazed facial expressions and animated dialect, but the novelty wears thin rather quickly. As best said by the actor himself, "Do not go in there."
