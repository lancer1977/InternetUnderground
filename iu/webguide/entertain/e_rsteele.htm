REMINGTON STEELE

In the days before Brosnan was Bond, there was Remington Steele, a modest-hit detective show where Brosnan and Stephanie Zimbalist solved mysteries by recounting old movie plots. A good show without much of an afterlife, Steele has found a home on the Web with episode guides, photos and good bios, proving once again that, thanks to the Web, nothing decent from the '80s need ever die.
