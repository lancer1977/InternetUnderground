NYPD BLANK

This short site is the only formula you'll need to create your own highly rated cop drama for network television. You are given the outline and the setup. All you have to do is flip a coin to choose the direction your story will take. The humorous mock-script provided is modeled after the ever- so-popular series NYPD Blue. The beauty here is that you don't even need to watch the show to write your own episode as the characters' names and modus operandi are clearly defined for you. Replete with commercial breaks.
