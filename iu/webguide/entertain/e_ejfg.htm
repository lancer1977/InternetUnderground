ENCYCLOPEDIC JAPANESE FILM GUIDE

While definitely a drab read with a simple gray background, black lettering and no visuals, this site is comprehensive and useful nonetheless. Dozens of Japanese films and documentary works are listed by title, short description, running time, video format, audience and production source. Entertainment films such as Tampopo are mentioned along with films such as Suburban Tokyo High School Students, the eastern version of Hoop Dreams undoubtedly. Also worth mentioning are the instructional Sumo and tea ceremony videos.
