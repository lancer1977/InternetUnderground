THE FACTS OF LIFE

"You take the good, you take the bad, you take 'em both and then you have..." The Facts of Life. This fan site is not yet completed, but oh! what plans are laid out for it. Currently in place are some of the episode guides, home pages for the main characters, a list of guest stars (would you believe Alan Alda, Helen Hunt and Mayim Bialik?) and our fave, the "Series Summary" which gives the lowdown on the Eastland School and Edna's Edibles. We always were looking for an explanation of why those chicks hung around for so many years...
