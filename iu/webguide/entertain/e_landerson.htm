LAURIE ANDERSON

Laurie Anderson and Voyager create a savvy coupling of pioneering talent and a knack for the indescribable. Cool graphics complement intriguing content, lists of performance dates, a question/hint section for the Puppet Motel journey, Quicktime movies and a complete description of the Theme Park concept Laurie and Peter Gabriel are tossing about. Don't miss the audio files in the listening room and the opportunity to get to know the crew and set of the Nerve Bible page. Includes links to Brian Eno's and Peter Gabriel's Web sites too.
