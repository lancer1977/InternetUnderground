PAPA PAGE

Like peeking through Ernest Hemingway's photo album, the Papa Page appears to be nothing but lots of images, but is actually a solid compilation of biographical information on the renowned writer. A chronological detail of the writer's life complements the photos dating from his childhood days in Oak Park, Ill., (which is also, notably, the home of IU art director Tom DeMay) to the ex-pat years in Europe and beyond. A simple hypertext list of contents makes maneuvering around this site a breeze. Of note are the links to other Hemingway sights and hangouts of old.
