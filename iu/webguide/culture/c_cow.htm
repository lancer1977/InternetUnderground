CASTLES ON THE WEB

This is the place for more castle information, images and trivia than you ever imagined existed. In addition to the weighty amount of content, the design wins accolades as well. The parchment paper look of the Web pages adds a Medieval feel to the site. The castle glossary will help you distinguish between a bivalate and a spandrel. If you're a king, queen, knight or rock star in the market for a castle of your own, the Webmaster even includes real estate listings.
