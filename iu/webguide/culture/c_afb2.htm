ADAM'S FOX BOX II

If you did a keyword search in Lycos for bikini-clad images referred to under the broad term "fox," you may be disappointed. If you are looking for four-legged furry creatures, stick around a while; you've found probably the best site for the topic -- at least the most thorough. Fables, stories, songs and lists of famous foxes or "Fox friends" are some of the categories in this Web tale. Let this be a lesson to keyword searchers; sometimes a cigar is just a cigar.
