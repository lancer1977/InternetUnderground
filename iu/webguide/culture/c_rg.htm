ROYAL GENEAOLOGIES

Tighten your corsets and dust your wigs as you follow the trail of mating cycles of the rich and highly-publicized blue-bloods in this hike up royal family trees. Numerous indexed links lead the reader to birth, death and marriage information of queens, kings and all the heirs they've spawned throughout the centuries. Occasionally, on a personal biography, a burial place or other interesting tidbit can be found to help enhance those crazed family vacations or drunken stupor road trips to tourist attractions. This site is easy to navigate and filled with completely useless information unless you are thinking of tracing your heritage back to medival times and think you might be able to cash in on the royal loot. A tech- happy nod goes to the site for breaking up the files into 25k sections for faster viewing.
