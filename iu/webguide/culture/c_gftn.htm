GREETINGS FROM THE NOTTES

Straight from Italy, grandpa and grandma Web have arrived; this site does everything but pinch your cheeks, thankfully. Grandma Notte serves up her recipe of the week and a table-side image of the Nottes' grandchildren adorns the top of the home page. Most pleasurable is the "Health Issues" for seniors section, relaying information about arthritis, gout and cholesterol to elderly or curious readers. No list, including the Nottes', would be replete without the ubiquitous links to the unusual, the search engines of choice and the "favorites." Brother John's war diary is a bountiful discovery and is readable in English and Italian. Mike, Alba and the grandchildren are so cool.
