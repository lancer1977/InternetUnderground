EUROPA

In case the whole concept of the European Union is news to you, let's clarify: The European Union doesn't do much more than allow free trade and cash flow throughout the 15 member countries. This site, however, sets its sights higher, aiming for monetary integration and the free movement of persons by the turn of the century. The best part by far, and the one with the most entertainment value for non-Europeans, is the Euromyths section -- it's nice to know that bureaucratic half-truths abound throughout the world.
