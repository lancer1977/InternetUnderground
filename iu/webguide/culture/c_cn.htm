CLIFFS NOTES

"So much to study, so little time." From the company that made school tolerable and studying the night before the test realistic, The Cliffs Notes Home Page may be a commercial site, but we figured the kids would appreciate it just the same. A complete listing of Cliffs Notes publications can be found by author and title, with samples of some content online. An FAQ and the opportunity to get a free screensaver can be found here as well. You can't order on the site; however, a toll-free telephone number and an order form are available online for you to expedite the purchasing process.
