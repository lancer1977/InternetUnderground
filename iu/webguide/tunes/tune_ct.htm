COCTEAU TWINS

This is an amazing discography of the Cocteau Twins, who are Robin Guthrie, Will Heggie and Elizabeth Fraser. Their ethereal sound and half-intelligible lyrics combine into works of great beauty and power, made most popular on Heaven or Las Vegas. This site is the definitive resource for learning about each and every track they've ever released, getting its history, authorship and track record -- and we're talking about scores and scores of songs.
