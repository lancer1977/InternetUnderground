THE DROWNSODA HOLE PAGES

Pouty and pretty with an angry nod to the ugliness in the world, both Courtney Love and tHE DrOwNSoDa page display a disconcerting blend of little-kid innocence and all-too-grown-up rottenness. This site has a meticulous collection of Hole's material and history, including a huge discography of legit albums, bootleg audio and video, songs Hole has covered, and tour dates and reviews. There are also many pictures of Hole, so you can get a load of the girl with the most cake and all her friends.
