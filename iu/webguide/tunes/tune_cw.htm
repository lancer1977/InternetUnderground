CRASH WORSHIP

Graphics from Crash Worship album covers are great to look at, but more band information and less eye candy would make this site much better. The band known for concerts that are more like audience participation and performance art and music more like "religious" experiences, has a stash of images, disturbing and funny. The site is attractive and the images are well-compressed. However, you may feel like you're lost in a dark tunnel at times. It's not the easiest site to get around because you proceed through the image files by clicking the "Next" button, not knowing exactly where you are going. We'll be your flashlight and tell you that you simply jump from image to image until there are no more.
