RANDY NEWMAN

You may know Randy Newman from his ubiquitous song, Short People (Got No Reason to Live) or the sarcastic I Love L.A., but we're here to tell you that you're missing some of the best the man has to offer. "Newman is one of the most sardonic, iconoclastic, musically adept and stylistically diverse singer/songwriters in the music industry...Like him or not, his songs, often character sketches drawn from our society's boorish, bigoted, born-again and often dangerous fringe, are likely to leave a lasting impression." In addition to his soulful, funny tunes, Newman has scored such soundtracks as Toy Story and The Natural. This site is a great introduction to Randy's work.
