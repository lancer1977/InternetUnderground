POE

We expected to find more of a tome considering the subject of this site, singer Poe, best known for the songs "Angry Johnny" and "Trigger Happy Jack," is a Net and computer chick. This Web page has nothing more than a biography and an interview from BigO magazine, but is the most complete Poe tribute we found. The page has one image of the songster diva, who played in a band while attending Princeton University, and an interview that reveals Poe's inspiration and motivation in writing and making music. Of note, Poe wrote a song titled, "Hello," which includes the lines, "M.O.D. are you out there? I can't see your face/But you left a trace on a data back-road that I almost erased." If you can't figure out what that's about, what are you doing on the Net begin with?
