GROUPIES HOME PAGE

Beavis and Butt-head would be surprised to find that this site does not live up to what could potentially be -- the title promises lots of skin and leather. The Groupies Home Page does little else than offer three e-mail addresses and a link to a home page belonging to a gorgeous groupie who models, writes books (and gets published too!) and has her own television show in Manhattan. Go backstage to La Floppy Disco to see what this site is all about. If you happen to be a groupie, you can submit your name and e-mail address or URL to be placed on the list of groupies. You may be shot down though, due to quality control. The groupies profess that "We are gourmets with only the finest on our menus."
