JUKEBOX FROM HELL©

The Jukebox From Hell is billed as the "ORIGINAL place for Mobile DJs and die-hard music fans to exchange memories of past ÎOne-Hit Wonders' or songs that just make you say ÎYou Never Hear That On The Radio Anymore.'" The Jukebox was good for sparking impassioned discussion and impromptu singalongs around the IU office, but woefully short on sound clips. We especially liked the trivia question, where you're called upon to identify a random sound clip.
