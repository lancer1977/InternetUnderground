COMBUSTIBLE EDISON

This site is a reminder of how we should ignore the bad things to see the good. The Combustible Edison site is a Sub Pop creation dedicated to the band responsible for the Four Rooms soundtrack. The site has loads of information and some really great photos, but it hasn't been updated in eons (In Webspeak, that means months! Egads!). You can find out about your fave CE performer such as Miss Lily Banquette, the Millionaire and so on. The discography contains some WAV files, but is mostly textual. Don't expect to learn much from the concert schedule, as it hasn't been updated since November of '95. Stop snoozing, guys. You're site is cool, but not THAT cool.
