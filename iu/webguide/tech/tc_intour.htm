INTERNET TOURBUS

If cybersurf is not your bag, kick back and cruise on the soul train of the Internet. "Why surf when you can ride the bus?" is the motto of this show which presents the passive side of the Web. Unlike most bus rides, you can safely interact with the other people on the bus and stop by their home pages. If you're not sold yet, there are no transfers, muggers, screaming children or Jack Daniels soloists on this ride--just you, the other tourists and the Tourbus guides.
