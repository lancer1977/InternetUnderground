THE ANIMAL ALPHABET

Humuhumu nukunuku apua'a? If you didn't know that this is the state fish of Hawaii, this site may be more useful to you than you think. The Animal Alphabet Page is part of a larger book site dedicated to critters. The Animal Alphabet is a simple list of links to pages that represent each letter of the alphabet and an animal name that starts with that letter. From squirrels and elephants to xuprocars and narwhals, this site not only gives you an image of the animal, it also teaches you about its habitat, lifestyle and more. The site is simple and straightforward.
