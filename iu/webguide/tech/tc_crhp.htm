CYBER-RIGHTS HOME PAGE

"The Campaign for Cyber-Rights seeks to raise awareness of the forces involved in the commercial development of Cyberspace, and to promote global support for the Declaration of Rights in Cyberspace. We see this as being crucial not only to the continuance of our open Internet culture, but to the survival of democracy itself in the globalized Information Age." The basic rights are: the right to assemble in online communities, to speak freely, to have privacy online and for networks to be priced reasonably. A good cause.
