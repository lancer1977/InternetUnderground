UNDERGROUND PROPAGANDA

"Truth in the guise of deception." Underground Propaganda is a site dedicated to the future of free speech, weeding through mediaspeak and philosophical issues dealing with the direction the Internet--and technology in general--is heading. The "Computer Underground" page contains a section of links to articles such as "In defense of piracy" and other sources on the Web including hacker merchandise (T-shirts, not blue boxes, sorry guys) and features about the abuse of privacy laws and so forth. How does the Webmaster feel about the future? "We are slowly being sucked into a surrealistic science-fiction novel."
