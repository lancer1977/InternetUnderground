TAP--THE ADA PROJECT

The Ada Project (TAP) is a volunteer project run by women at Yale for the purpose of consolidating the Web's scattered resources on women in computing. This site is informative as well as inspirational; you'll find bios of prominent women in computing, conferences, online research papers, employment links, fellowship and grant info, discussion groups and statistics. We got the biggest kick out of the historical photos of women interacting with various computers.
