PARAGLIDING

Even if paragliding isn't the most common activity, the depth of its site on the Web makes it seem as popular as football. Worth visiting for the pictures alone, this page also includes an in-depth accident log intended to help paragliders avoid similar mistakes, though it serves just as well as an honest warning for potentially reckless paragliders. Though the page does provide a decent intro for the neophyte, you'll be much more at home among its resources if you're a paragliding devotee. Still, there are some pretty swell videos.
