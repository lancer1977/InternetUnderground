HASHI JAPANESE CHOPSTICKS

Oh sure, put a monkey on the home page and watch us marvel. The Hashi site is more than an image of a monkey holding chopsticks. Here, you'll not only learn the appropriate way to handle chopsticks yourself, you'll also learn how using them develops a person's mind and motor skills. Once you learn how to hold the sticks, this site will guide you all the way through to the refined "etiquette" section. "Skewer chopsticks" and "waving-in-the-air chopsticks" are two taboos you won't want to be accused of by chopstick aficionados. The site is loaded with images of different types of sticks and accessories. Viewable in both and English and in Japanese.
