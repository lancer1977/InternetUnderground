AMI SKI NEWS

In case you're looking for slopes to hit over the weekend, AMI (the company that provides reports for all those other mediums like TV and radio) has built a large and comprehensive resource to check out ski conditions at mountains all over the U.S. AMI has even added RealAudio ski reports for various regions, which, combined with other weather links, gives just about any skier more than enough information before he or she loads up the station wagon. A thoroughly useful site.
