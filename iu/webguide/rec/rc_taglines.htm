TAGLINES GALORE

This collection of more than 59,000 taglines, otherwise known as catchy little slogans that quickly become overused, was compiled by the Webmaster through contributions from the Internet community. Taglines from car acronyms (such as Pinto: Put In New Transmission Often) to book titles are listed in huge quantities. If the primary list of taglines isn't enough, 100 random lines are highlighted each day. A lot of these lines are pretty lame and sound like marketing slogans, but if you look carefully, there are some entries worthy of note.
