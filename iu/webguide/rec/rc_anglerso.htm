ANGLERS ONLINE

If flyfishing is not something you think you'll find in alt.sex, you should check out this site. The fishing yellow pages are full of links to gear, trip planning and just about anything you'd want to know about this centuries-old sport. Join the fisherman's, or excuse us, fisherperson's chat room or take advantage of the Anglers online search engine, optimizing your time on the waterfront and minimizing your time on the keyboard. Whatever you do, don't forget to submit your "Big one" photo to the scrapbook.
