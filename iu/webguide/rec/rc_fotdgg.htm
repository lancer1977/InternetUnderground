FAKE OUT! THE DEFINITION GUESSING GAME

Do you know a funambulist from a silviculture? What about a bunyip from a chickaree? The "Fake Out!" site is an educational Web page that allows you to match a given term to the correct definition. The incentive here is that you can submit your own "fake" definition to be added to the list to "Fake Out" other people playing the game. This may sound like a skill-building game for adults, but surprisingly the categories are listed for kindergarten through sixth grade students and older. Should we have paid more attention in grade school or what?
