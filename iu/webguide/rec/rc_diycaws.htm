DO IT YOURSELF COUNTRY AND WESTERN SONG

Mad-Libs meets Garth Brooks. This site is a single page of text dedicated to helping you create your own country/western lyrics. For example, the first line reads, "I met her ____1____ ..." For blank number one, you are given several options to choose from, such as "Outside Fresno, at a truck stop or in a nightmare." Each of the remaining 11 spaces have similar lines to choose from to complete your song. The problem here is that this site is not interactive. It is designed for you to print up and pass around the office or give to your wife, provided you actually have a job or a spouse.
