BUZBEE BAT HOUSE TEMPERATURE PLOT

Webmaster Jim Buzbee of Colorado created this Web site to share his experiences in the bat-wrangling profession. Buzbee's contribution to the Web is a chart that tracks a five-day temperature within the bat house he constructed. The Webmaster did not own any bats before he built the house and as his site mentions, he hasn't attracted any yet. But he is getting the temperature theory down to a science. Numerous links to other bat sites are included. The temperature gauge is one thing, but wait until you see the poetry, articles, images and bat jokes this site connects you with. You'd have to lock yourself up in a cave to be able to consume all of this data.
