INTERNET BARTENDER'S GUIDE

This unofficial, textual guide to mixing drinks is a great resource for bartenders, dorm residents and pretty much anyone with an interest in alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages. The table of contents includes drinking games, measurements, an introduction to mixology, overindulgence information and an alphabetical list of drinks. You can scroll through the site or use the "find" feature to get to the drink or section you're interested in. The site is huge, and contans many unusual drink recipes that, if printed, could be very useful stored near your wet bar. Unless you drink at your keyboard.
