MERRILL SHINDLER'S GUIDE TO EATING PRETTY GOOD

Shindler's list includes recipes, food facts, surveys and tips on where to find the best steak in the U.S. The Los Angeles Zagat Survey author and host of KABC talkradio's "Dining Out With Merrill Shindler Show," also hosts this FoodPlex site, describing himself as, "Just a big ole hungry boy," serving up lots of food for thought. A balance of graphics and content make this site entertaining, educational and a wee bit campy. We can't overlook the highlights though. A breakdown of the "distribution of letters in alphabet soup" and "foreign foods that aren't foreign" are two appetizers you'll sample here.
