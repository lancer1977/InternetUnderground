SIMCITY 2000 RESOURCE GUIDE

Awesome guide for that addictive little power trip known as Maxis's SimCity 2000. This page provides FAQs of the game and the add-on packs. Learn advanced strategies on how to stimulate your Sim economy, use the cleanest power and make your clamoring citizens happy. The page also lists bugs, updates and cheats; yes, it's true that there's a way to get free money. A Maxis employee is online answering questions, and there's a link to their help desk. All in all, a fun, incredibly detailed companion to the game.
