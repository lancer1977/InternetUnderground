NIAGARAVATION

Billed as "staggeringly funny," Niagaravation tells the touching tale of a couple trying to stop aliens from abducting Niagara Falls in 1961. We spent a fair amount of time trying to figure out if this was a real movie or not, which is probably not a good sign -- in any case, it's a pretty mediocre pitch. A JPEG, a few sound bites, maybe even a plot synopsis: anything to capture our attention other than a distinct surplus of exclamation points. At least the link to the live Niagara Falls cam works.
