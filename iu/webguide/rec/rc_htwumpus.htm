HUNT THE WUMPUS

In this multiplayer game, the wumpus is a cave-dwelling creature that you are to hunt and kill. The prey is "sleeping" and not visible when you arrive at the site, so you must choose which cave, as designated by number and image, to shoot your "arrow" into. You are only given five magic arrows, so choose carefully. Your risks include: getting eaten, being shot by another hunter or your own ricocheting arrow, shooting a fellow hunter or "getting old" by not moving fast enough. We never saw the wumpus, but we shot ourselves to the death. Good luck.
