ADZE MIXXE, ASTROLOGER

AdZeMiXXe is a man with a mission: To spread the good word of astrology and his unique sense of capitalization across the globe. The site isn't just a listing of daily horoscopes -- the kind you'd find in the local paper -- but rather a complete astrological forecast, filled iwth unusual advice. The cool thing is that if you have no interest in your own destiny, you can glance at the star charts of horoscopes of celebrities themselves! A tip for Geraldo reads, "Work on your smile." David Letterman's tip is to "learn a new dance step and show off your magic feet." This site may not change your life (and will undoubtedly not do much to change or better your opinions of any celebrities), but it will keep you entertained, and that's what "stars" are for anyway, right?
