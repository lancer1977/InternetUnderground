THE OPEN SCROLL

Your source for political discourse and the source for previously unpublished work. This stock house of poetry, short stories and other expressions is heavy on imagery and loaded with content. A great feature is the poetry exchange; submit your thoughts ad infinitum and they will appear in their original incarnation for thousands to see. Be warned. Don't start dumping your high-school poetry class journal, a plethora of phrases such as, "Your heart cuts through my soul like an obsidian dagger," already exist here. Exercise your freedom of expression with caution and concern for the artistic community around you, please.
