ZIPZAP

Two 'zines for the price of one here. Along with ZIPZAP, Williams & Fenn Publications has included Deep Style, which is a paper publication as well, to the list of things to check out. ZIPZAP has the content and Deep Style has flash but doesn't really ask the "big" questions. The home page is a bit confusing. You may actually jump around this site for a while before you figure out which 'zine you're reading. We spent some time flipping back and forth, reading poetry and fiction at ZIPZAP and snagging some eye-candy at "America's non-fiction fashion magazine" and determined that the two should probably merge so that the content 'zine can look better and the pretty 'zine can say something. Hey, your chocolate's in my peanut butter.
