dEPARTURE fROM nORMAL

dEPARTURE fROM nORMAL is a free 'zine of art stuff. dfn publishes original photos, drawings, paintings, stories, poems, music, animation, and any other form of art submitted that can be digitized. "Our mission statement could be this: We do everything because we want to. We accept everything, as long as we like it. We offer everything we accept, and nothing we do not accept."
