How do you spend offline time?

Editing our magazine, hiding from ringing phones, playing with my recalcitrant cat, observing things, listening to my friends' woes and writing stories.

I'm offended.


What was the first site that really caught your attention?

I'll always have fond memories of my first visit to the "Unofficial Facts of Life Home Page." What a hoot it was reliving those timeless (and, yes, touching) moments in Blair, Joe, Natalie, Tootie and Mrs. G's lives!


What scares you about the Internet?

I've discovered that I'm sharing a planet with people who have self-appointed sobriquets such as "SukMyToes" and "GenXer"


What best prepared you for your work?

18+ years of living with my parents sufficiently deadened my senses enough to render me useless to everyone but the IU staff. Thanks, Mom and Dad!


Are your a dog or cat person? Or perhaps some alternative pet?

Ever since the hunting-accident death of my beloved crocodile, Bologna, and the still-unexplained garbage disposal death of my eight piranhas (named by my father after my mother's siblings), I haven't been able to handle the emotional instability of pet ownership.
