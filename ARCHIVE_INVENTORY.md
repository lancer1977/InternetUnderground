# Archive Inventory

This repository preserves a static copy of the original `Internet Underground`
site under `iu/`.

## Top-Level Items

- `index.html`: current repo landing page
- `README.md`: project framing and preservation notes
- `iu/`: preserved archive of the original site
- `navart/`: supporting artwork used by the repo landing page
- `u-o.gif`: landing page artwork

## Major Archive Areas

- `iu/index.html`: archive entry point and archive statement
- `iu/contact/`: contact pages, staff pages, and related artwork
- `iu/contest/`: contest pages and contest art
- `iu/webguide/`: web guide pages, culture pages, and navigation art

## Asset Types

- HTML and HTM pages
- GIF, JPG, and PNG-style static artwork
- Navigation art and page chrome
- Legacy inline scripts and external ad or analytics references

## Observations

- The archive is broad and heavily image-driven.
- Many filenames are short, legacy-era, and tied to page-local navigation.
- The site mixes preserved internal content with external references for ads
  and analytics.

## Known Gaps

This pass did not identify a confirmed missing-file list from the top-level
inspection alone. Any missing asset findings should be recorded in
`BROKEN_LINKS.md` as they are discovered.
