This site was coded entirely by hand (all 1000+ files) — using Notepad.exe — between January 1996 and May 1997. All maintenance pre-1997 was done over a 33k modem. What larks.
Internet Underground Magazine was created by Sendai Publishing Group, which was bought by Ziff-Davis Publishing in mid 1996.
The magazine was shut down midway through production of its 19th edition. All stories and art were killed.
The contents of this site are the product of the former staff and contributors, and I intend the existence of this archive to celebrate the work they did, and the work that I was allowed to do.
